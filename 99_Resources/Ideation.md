
# Ideation

| Need | Key User | Environment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Improve solar energy collection by automatically tracking the sun. | Homeowners and small solar system users. | Outdoor areas with direct sunlight and changing sun position during the day. |
| Reduce the need for manual solar panel adjustment. | People using portable or off-grid solar panels. | Remote or outdoor locations where frequent manual adjustment is inconvenient. |
| Provide more efficient renewable energy use for small-scale applications. | Students, hobbyists, campers, and remote users. | Outdoor conditions with weather exposure, sunlight variation, and day-long operation. |
